Brand Strategy — AISocial

🗺️ Launch Roadmap

Phase 1
Weeks 1–4

Foundation — Character & Content System

Finalize all 5 character identities, voice profiles, and visual avatars. Build the script generation pipeline. Create 3 pilot episodes across different formats. Establish the iDocSocial integration pathway.

Phase 2
Weeks 5–8

Launch — Multi-Platform Rollout

Deploy Weekly Eye News as flagship series. Publish Hot Takes 3x/week. Launch Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube channels. Begin audience building with ophthalmology KOL engagement. Nexus automates content pipeline.

Phase 3
Weeks 9–16

Growth — Audience & Engagement

Introduce Case Battles and Conference Recaps. Launch "Follow Your Personality" subscription model (Team Refractive, Team Evidence, Team Conservative). Cross-promote on iDocSocial. Target 10K followers across platforms.

Phase 4
Month 5+

Monetize — Sponsors & Scale

Approach industry sponsors with proven engagement data. Launch sponsored segments (Refractive → laser platforms, Retina → drug pipelines). Explore AI thought leader positioning — characters become recognized voices in ophthalmology media.
